Benefits

Satellite internet deployment in India faces a combination of high costs, significant technical limitations (like weather interference,) and a complex regulatory environment. These challenges impact affordability, reliability, and national security considerations

Key Benefits for Satellite Internet in India

Universal Coverage

Satellite internet can provide access virtually anywhere, including remote villages, islands, high-altitude regions like the Himalayas, and even at sea or in the air, overcoming geographical obstacles that hinder the laying of physical cables.

Rapid Deployment

Unlike fiber networks, which require extensive and time-consuming infrastructure development, satellite internet systems can be deployed relatively quickly with minimal ground setup (just a user terminal/dish).

Technological Advancements

The shift to Low Earth Orbit (LEO) and Medium Earth Orbit (MEO) satellites, utilized by providers like Starlink and JioSpaceFiber, significantly reduces latency compared to older technology, making real-time applications like video calls more feasible.
